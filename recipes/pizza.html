Pizza

Description

Follow along this recipe to create a delicious pepperoni pizza in your very own home.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (see below)
  • 18-20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 oz grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Pizza Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (no salt)
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp granulated garlic
  • 1/2 tsp granulated onion
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F
  2. Make sauce by combining pizza sauce ingredients (shown above) and stirring
  3. Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface (~14 in diameter)
  4. Transfer dough into a large cast-iron skillet
  5. Add the toppings (sauce, grated cheese, pepperoni and black pepper)
  6. Place skillet over high heat burner for 2 mins, then transfer to 500°F oven and bake for 10-12 mins
  7. Let pizza rest for 1 min, then season with fresh oregano and slice into pieces
